Kids Novice Bout Competition at Hit Fit Melton

Join us at Hit Fit Melton for an exciting Kids Novice Bout Competition! Young aspiring boxers will step into the ring to showcase their skills, determination, and sportsmanship. This event provides a fantastic opportunity for children to experience the thrill of boxing in a safe and supportive environment. Cheer on our young athletes as they compete in spirited bouts, making memories and building confidence. Don’t miss this fun-filled day of boxing for the whole family!
